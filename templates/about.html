ABOUT TAWA COUNTRY LODGE

This lovely and tranquil country home is situeated next to Kooi earth dam in the outskirts of Tawa Market in Makueni County. It's a perfect gateway for the weekend as it is far from the hustle and bustle fo city life. The house consists of 5 self-contained and well-appointed bedrooms, each with a TV, bar fridge and coffee making facilities. The House is also has a common lounge (with TV) that flows to a veranda overlloking the garden. Here guests can sit and have breakfast or just lounge as they enjoy a sundowner later in the day. Rates for accommodation on a bed and breakfast basis Single occupancy: Ksh. 6,000 per night Double occupancy: Ksh 7,000 per night Group bookings: Ksh 25,000 per night for maximum of 10 persons. Breakfast is served from 7:00 am on weekdays and 8:00 am on weekends but we are always willing to accommodate you on request. A cook may also be hired on the request at a cost of Kshs 2,000 to 3,000. Alternatively, You may also opt to prepare your own meals in the kitchen (T&Cs apply). Enquire during reservations on 0720-162-297/ 0787-621-382/ 0721-637-491 Activities on offer, A tour of the garden. If you are lucky you may just find a fruit to eat on the way! A walk around dam, Bird watching, Picnic arranged on request at a site across the dam, Snacks and Drinks can be provided at an additional cost. Tawa market is about 5 Kilometres away and offers basic shopping. Checking in: from 12 noon. Check out: 11 am.
